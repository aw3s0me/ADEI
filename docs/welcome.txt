 Dear user, welcome to the Advanced Data Extraction Infrastructure! 
The ADEI is loaded and ready to work. 
Please, click on "Data Source" popup on the left sidebar and select the data
source and time interval. Alternatively, you could use a pulldown menu on the 
top of sidebar.


Navigation trough the data is feasible with mouse. Please, press and hold
left mouse button to select region. Then click on the arrow button to zoom
inside. The mouse wheel could be used to shift current window left


To download selected data open 'Controls' popup and choose "Export" tab. 
You will be able to get the data in the multiple supported formats. 
Please, Be patient, the preparation of data may take a while.


Please, to find more information about data navigation and export go to the 
Help page.
